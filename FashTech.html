Interactive Fashion

As a creative I have always been intersted in exploring opportunities to innovate otherwise mundane everyday experiences, I find that there is potential all around us waiting to be explored.

This is the reason why I use research and conceptual thinking to create solutions that will hopefully enhance the users relationships to consumer products and services.

These methods are applied while assisting ethical and innovative processes during their development/delivery to bridge traditional manufacturing with disruptive technologies.

Bachelors Thesis Fashion Design + Business


"Home is where the hearth is, where the love where the light is...

Home is where I am when I am with you".


By connecting the sense of physicallity with the sense of belonging a connection was created between digital and physical mapping of social connections.

Furthermore the collection was created combining classic craftmensgip with modern variations of emboirdery, 3D printing and up-cycling.

Scanned tags would also help set the mood by bringing the wearer to a collection inspired playlist.

"I am You": Interactive Fashion Collection


In a country as international as The Netherlands, cities such as Amsterdam have become examples of mixed cultural interactions on a a daily basis.

I am you explores the universal connections between women rather than the differences, women with of different ages, cultural background and believes where interviewed.

The three main subjects, culture, empworment and diversity.

These messages were embedded into the prints of the collection pieces using Augmented reality.

Each garment had a hidde message printed on them that could be unlocked by using your phone to show the wearer that they are not alone as you are me and "I am you".

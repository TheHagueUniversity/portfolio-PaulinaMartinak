Outcomes & Learnings

Ever since I first started studying I went back and forward between Design and Technology.

This why I love how the two are becoming more interlinked than ever before.

Throughout the years I have come to see that my strength lies in development stages of the creative process.

Becoming a UX-design designer is a great way to further develop my ressearch skills while still actively work with interactive technolgies. I am happy to share my first results and learnings with you. Let's have look...

What I am learning...

Competences practiced as UX-researcher so far.

Having the ability to develop my professional skills within the world of UX-design.

All and all during my first projects as an UX-design student, I have found my strengts in the first three parts of the design thinking process. In the future I am looking forward to learning more about the prototyping and testing fase of concept and product development.
Right now I am very happy to be challenging myself and my way of thinking being part of such a multicultural group, as it allows me to stay curious, ask questions while leting go of any and all judgements.
The projects so far have been a nice refresher of working within groups of different ages. I have learned to better balance my time management. I have also been reminded that "More roads can lead to rome" all the while remaining authentics to what make my way unique.
So far I have been able to practice my professional skills, learn about my personal strengths and points of improvement. It is a fun ride and I am looking forward to more adventures. Want to collaborate and grow together?
